Instructions
Before you begin

This form documents suspected abuse, neglect, or exploitation. Complete it as soon as possible after a disclosure or observation. Use the child’s own words where possible. Do not investigate. Report directly to local child welfare authorities. See SG-POL-004 — Reporting & Response.

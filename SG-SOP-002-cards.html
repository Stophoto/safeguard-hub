Safeguard · Standard Operating Procedure
Washroom Escort
Protocol
The Core Rule · Non-negotiable
No adult is ever alone with a child in a washroom. Two adults escort, every time.
Protocol · when a child needs the washroom
  1. 1
    Pause & pair up.Acknowledge the child. Signal a second approved volunteer to escort with you. Never proceed alone.
  2. 2
    Take a buddy child if possible.Escort two children together when you can; a single child only goes with two adults.
  3. 3
    Walk — don't enter.Walk the child to the washroom. Both adults wait outside the door, within sight of each other.
  4. 4
    Door open, stall closed.Prop the washroom door open. The child uses a closed stall independently.
  5. 5
    Check in verbally.If the child takes longer than 2 minutes, knock and speak from outside the door. Do not enter.
  6. 6
    Return & log.Walk the child back to the classroom. Note the escort on the session check-sheet.
Never · under any circumstance
  • Enter a stall while a child is inside it.
  • Escort a child to the washroom alone — even briefly, even with a parent's verbal OK.
  • Close the outer washroom door behind you.
  • Assist with clothing or toileting without the second adult present and visible.
If the child needs physical assistance Stop. Call the parent or guardian to the washroom to assist. If unreachable, call the Safeguard Coordinator. Both adults remain present throughout.

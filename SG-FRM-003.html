Police Check Letter & Consent

Volunteer Police Information Check — Vulnerable Sector Search
Document Code: SG-FRM-003 Version: 2.0 Issued: March 2026 Confidential — Screening Purposes Only
Checklist
Progress tracker

Both the applicant and the ministry leader must complete their respective steps. Use this checklist to track progress.

  • Leader issues cover letter (Section B) on church letterhead and signs it
  • Applicant goes online to apply for Police Information Check (see Section A)
  • Applicant uploads cover letter during online application
  • Applicant signs Consent Form (Section C) and returns it to leader
  • Police Check is submitted online by applicant
  • Results are received directly by the church
  • Screening personnel confirm results have been reviewed and filed
Section A
Instructions to Applicant

All volunteers serving with children, youth, or vulnerable persons must complete a Police Information Check (PIC) with Vulnerable Sector Search through the local police service.

Online Application

Go to: https://policeinformationcheck.calgarypolice.ca

Type this address exactly if the link is not clickable.

When applying:

  • Select Volunteer Police Information Check with Vulnerable Sector Search.
  • Upload two pieces of government-issued ID (one must include photo).
  • Enter your church name as the organization.
  • Upload the cover letter you receive from your leader (see Section B).

Once you apply, the police service will process your application. Results will be delivered digitally to the church.

You must also sign the Consent Form (Section C) so the church can store your results.

Section B
Cover Letter

Leaders: Print this on church letterhead, fill in the applicant’s details, and sign it. Give the signed letter to the applicant so they can upload it during their online application.

To: Calgary Police Service — Police Information Check Unit

Re: Volunteer Police Information Check — Vulnerable Sector Screening

This letter confirms that the following individual has applied to serve as a volunteer with our church, in a role involving children, youth, or vulnerable persons. We request that this applicant be processed under the reduced volunteer fee category.

We understand that all records provided are confidential and will be used solely for volunteer screening, in accordance with our Safeguard Framework (SG-POL-001 — Screening & Approval).

Sincerely,

Section C
Consent Form

Applicants: Sign this form and return it to your ministry leader. Do not upload this to the police service.

hereby authorize:

  • The local police service to conduct a Police Information Check including Vulnerable Sector Search.
  • The release of the results of this check directly to my church.
  • The church to store this record in my confidential ministry personnel file.

I understand that:

  • Any information obtained will be kept strictly confidential.
  • This check must be renewed every 3–5 years (per SG-POL-001 — Screening & Approval).
  • I may be required to provide fingerprints if requested by the police service.

When you reach capacity
  1. Stop admitting children. Hold your posted ratio and room capacity — never accept “just one more.”
  2. Call staff: page staff on the walkie-talkie, or flag the hallway floater to relay the message.
  3. Wait for staff. They decide whether to move some children to another room, send more volunteers, or have check-in contact parents.
  4. Remind check-in if needed: new families for this age group should be told there is no room until staff resolve it.
  5. Resume admitting children only when staff confirm capacity is safe.

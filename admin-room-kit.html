Classroom Kit

Every printable in one place — room posters, quick-reference cards, and procedure summaries designed to be printed, laminated, and mounted in each ministry space. Use this at the start of each ministry year, or whenever a room needs a fresh set.

Suggested rhythm: print the full kit before your fall ministry launch, and anytime a new classroom is opened or signage is damaged. Review the policy doc behind each item before printing so you know what you’re posting.

Police Check — Cover Letter

For coordinators only. The volunteer never fills this in — they only receive it. Print it on church letterhead, fill in the applicant's details, sign it, and give the signed letter to the applicant so they can upload it during their online application (it unlocks the reduced volunteer fee).

To: Calgary Police Service — Police Information Check Unit

Re: Volunteer Police Information Check — Vulnerable Sector Screening

This letter confirms that the following individual has applied to serve as a volunteer with our church, in a role involving children, youth, or vulnerable persons. We request that this applicant be processed under the reduced volunteer fee category.

We understand that all records provided are confidential and will be used solely for volunteer screening, in accordance with our Safeguard Framework (SG-POL-001 — Screening & Approval).

Sincerely,

Worker’s Covenant & Annual Acknowledgement

Initial Commitment and Annual Policy Renewal
Document Code: SG-FRM-004 Version: 2.0 Issued: March 2026 Confidential — Ministry Personnel File

This form serves two purposes: (1) as the initial covenant every worker signs upon approval, and (2) as the annual acknowledgement renewed each ministry year. Both signatures are collected on this single document.

Section 1
Worker Information
Section 2
Commitment to Child Protection

As a ministry worker, I affirm that:

  • I have read and understood the Safeguard Framework’s core protection policies (SG-POL-001 through SG-POL-004) and the governance policy (SG-G-001).
  • I will abide by these policies at all times in my ministry role.
  • I understand that failure to follow these policies may result in removal from ministry.
Section 3
Commitment to Personal Conduct

I agree to:

  • Live in a way that reflects the character of Christ, showing integrity, humility, and respect for others.
  • Abstain from any behavior that could reasonably be seen as abusive, exploitative, or inappropriate toward children, youth, or vulnerable persons.
  • Report any suspected abuse, neglect, or misconduct immediately, in accordance with SG-POL-004 — Reporting & Response.
  • Participate in required training, renewals, and refresher sessions as outlined in SG-G-001.
Section 4
Confidentiality

I understand that in serving I may learn personal information about children, youth, families, or ministry workers. I commit to keeping all such information confidential, except where reporting obligations require disclosure.

Section 5
Initial Covenant

I affirm my agreement with this covenant and commit myself to serving faithfully and responsibly.

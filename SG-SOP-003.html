Ratio & Room Capacity Card

Two-adult floor. Age-based ratios. Every room, every time.
Document Code: SG-SOP-003 Version: 2.0 Issued: March 2026
Always two adults minimum (Two-Adult Rule), regardless of group size. These ratios apply in classrooms, on trips, and at events.

Capacity
Room Capacity
  • Maximum children = number of adults × age-group ratio.
  • When maximum is reached: post a “Room Full” sign and redirect children to another class or notify the ministry leader.
Rules
Quick Rules
  • If a helper leaves the room, ratios must still be maintained by the remaining adults.
  • Youth helpers (under 18) may assist but do not count toward the two-adult minimum. Once the two-adult floor is met, youth helpers count toward ratio calculations.
  • Going above ratio requires a documented reason in SG-OPS-001 (Admin Guide, Exception Log).

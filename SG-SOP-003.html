SG-SOP-003  Standard Operating Procedure

Ratio & Room Capacity Card

Two-adult floor. Age-based ratios. Every room, every time.
Document Code: SG-SOP-003 Version: 2.0 Issued: March 2026
Always two adults minimum (Two-Adult Rule), regardless of group size. These ratios apply in classrooms, on trips, and at events.
Classroom Posters
Print the laminated wall-poster cards for each room.

One print run produces all five room posters — Nursery, Toddlers, Preschool, Elementary, Youth — each showing that room’s two-adult minimum, per-adult ratio, and max kids on one landscape sheet. Laminate and mount in every classroom.

Capacity
Room Capacity
  • Maximum children = number of adults × age-group ratio.
  • Children are checked in at the desk before service, so room counts are tracked at check-in. When a room reaches its maximum, stop admitting children and page staff — on the walkie-talkie or by flagging the hallway floater. Staff decide whether to move some children to another room, send more volunteers, or have check-in contact parents. (See the Capacity / Page-Staff sign.)
Rules
Quick Rules
  • If a helper leaves the room, ratios must still be maintained by the remaining adults.
  • Junior Volunteers (under 16) may assist but do not count toward the two-adult minimum. Once the two-adult floor is met, Junior Volunteers count toward ratio calculations.
  • Going above ratio requires a documented reason in SG-OPS-001 (Admin Guide, Exception Log).

How Safeguard Hub handles your information

Safeguard Hub is used by Bethany Chapel to protect children, youth, vulnerable adults, volunteers, and families. This page explains what we collect, who can see it, and how long we keep it.

What We Collect

Depending on your role and the forms you complete, Safeguard Hub may collect:

  • Name, date of birth, address, phone, email, and emergency contact.
  • Ministry preferences and availability.
  • Training records and onboarding status.
  • Police-check submission and clearance dates. We do not store the police check itself in Safeguard Hub.
  • Reference names, contact information, and reference status.
  • Worker's Covenant signing history.
  • Incident reports and suspected abuse reports.

Where It Is Stored

Safeguard Hub stores data in Google Firebase / Cloud Firestore, in the Firebase project safeguard-hub-71292. Data is stored in Google data centers.

No AI service touches this data. Safeguard Hub does not send volunteer or report data to OpenAI, ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or any other AI tool.

We do not use third-party processors for Safeguard Hub data beyond Google Firebase / Firestore.

Who Sees What

  • Volunteers can see their own profile, training, covenant, and onboarding records.
  • Coordinators can see volunteer profiles, training, screening, references, and ordinary form submissions.
  • Safeguard Leads can see suspected abuse reports. Coordinators who are not Safeguard Leads do not see suspected abuse reports.
  • No one outside Bethany Chapel staff has access to Safeguard Hub.

How Long We Keep Records

Our retention rules are documented in RETENTION.md.

Suspected abuse reports are kept until the subject reaches age 25, plus 7 years. These reports are placed under legal hold by default and are not automatically deleted.

Your Rights

You can ask for a copy of your Safeguard Hub information. You can also ask us to correct or delete information where deletion is allowed.

Some records cannot be deleted on request. For example, suspected abuse reports under legal hold may need to be kept for safety, reporting, insurance, or legal reasons.

Privacy Contact

Email safeguard@bethanychapel.ca with privacy questions or requests.

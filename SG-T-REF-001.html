Part 3

Confidentiality & Storage

Abuse reports (SG-FRM-007): Safeguard Team, Elders, and civil authorities only. Never share with other volunteers, parents of other children, or staff outside the reporting chain.

Incident reports (SG-FRM-006): Share with the parents of the child involved. Do not share details with other families.

Screening files: Contain personal information including criminal record checks. Stored securely by the Safeguard Team. Ministry Leaders may view covenant and application status but not police check results or reference details.

Variance logs: Operational transparency tool. Not to be used as a disciplinary record unless a pattern leads to a formal conversation documented separately.

Storage Requirements

Physical records: locked filing cabinet accessible only to the Safeguard Team.

Digital records: password-protected folder with access restricted by role.

When a volunteer leaves: retain their screening file and any incident records for the specified retention period. Do not destroy records early.

Safeguard · Standard Operating Procedure
Emergency & Injury
Protocols
Call 911
Evacuation
Fire · gas · unsafe building
  • Line children up at the door.
  • Take attendance clipboard or class list.
  • Exit by posted route to muster point.
  • Take attendance again.
  • Report missing persons immediately.
Lockdown
Intruder · external threat
  • Secure children inside the room.
  • Lock doors, turn off lights.
  • Silence devices; keep children quiet.
  • Stay away from windows.
  • Open only after official all-clear.
Serious injury
Head injury · fracture · unconscious
  • Do not move child unless danger remains.
  • Call 911 if needed.
  • Notify elder or senior staff.
  • Complete SG-FRM-006 within 24 hours.
Minor injury
Scrape · bruise · small cut · nosebleed
  • Provide basic first aid.
  • Notify ministry leader.
  • Complete SG-FRM-006.
  • Inform parent/guardian at pickup.
Coordinator review
After emergency or serious injury
  • Safeguard Coordinator reviews incident.
  • Document lessons learned.
  • Update procedures if needed.
In a crisis
Do not improvise alone. Keep children supervised, call for help, document before you leave.

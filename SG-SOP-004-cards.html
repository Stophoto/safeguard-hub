Safeguard · Standard Operating Procedure
Reporting
Flowchart
Every incident · Every time
Ensure safety first. Then report, document, and hand off. Do not investigate suspected abuse.
1
Make the child safe.
Remove immediate danger if possible. Provide first aid or call 911 when urgent help is needed.
2
Decide what happened.
Incident or injury: use SG-FRM-006. Suspected abuse or neglect: use SG-FRM-007 and report externally.
3
Notify ministry leadership.
Tell the ministry leader or Safeguard Coordinator immediately. Do not confront the alleged abuser.
4
Make required external reports.
For suspected abuse, report directly to Child & Family Services or Police. Do not notify parents if a parent is the suspected abuser.
5
Document the same day.
Complete the correct form before you leave. Report facts only. Include exact words where possible.
Incident / injury
Use SG-FRM-006
Notify parent/guardian promptly. Coordinator reviews and follows up.
Suspected abuse
Use SG-FRM-007
Report to authorities. Do not investigate. Do not promise secrecy.
Legal duty in Alberta
You are never in trouble for reporting in good faith. If you are unsure whether something is reportable, report upward immediately and seek direction from the Safeguard Lead.

Safeguard Framework · Standard Operating Procedure

Reporting Flowchart

Every incident. Every time. No skipped steps.
Document Code: SG-SOP-004 Version: 2.0 Issued: March 2026
Clear, step-by-step response to incidents, accidents, or suspected abuse. Follow this flowchart every time. Do not skip steps.
Step 1
Ensure Safety
  • Remove the child from immediate danger if applicable.
  • Provide first aid or call 911 if needed.
▼
Step 2
Determine Type
  • Accident / Injury / Behaviour → Use SG-FRM-006 (Incident Report).
  • Suspected Abuse / Neglect → Use SG-FRM-007 (Suspected Abuse Report).
▼
Step 3
Notify Leadership
  • Immediately inform the ministry leader or Safeguard Coordinator.
  • Do not investigate. Do not confront the alleged abuser.
▼
Step 4
External Reporting
  • Accidents/Injuries: notify parent/guardian promptly.
  • Suspected Abuse: report directly to Child & Family Services or Police.
  • Do not notify parents if the parent is the suspected abuser.
▼
Step 5
Document
  • Complete the appropriate form (SG-FRM-006 or SG-FRM-007) the same day.
  • File securely with the Safeguard Coordinator.
Reporting suspected abuse is a legal duty in Alberta. Report facts only. Never promise a child secrecy. You are never in trouble for reporting in good faith.

SG-SOP-007  Standard Operating Procedure

Transportation & Off-Site Safety

Two approved adults per vehicle. Every trip. Every time.
Document Code: SG-SOP-007 Version: 2.0 Issued: March 2026
Conditional SOP — applies only when the church runs a regular transportation ministry or organized off-site events. The Two-Adult Rule applies in every vehicle carrying minors or vulnerable adults.
Pre-Trip
Before the Trip
  1. All drivers approved via SG-FRM-010 (Driver Application). No exceptions.
  2. All drivers have completed full Safeguard screening (SG-POL-001).
  3. Signed parent permission (SG-FRM-009, Event Permission) on file for every minor.
  4. Trip plan completed on SG-FRM-011 (Transportation Log) and submitted to ministry leader.
  5. Vehicle is safe, insured, and equipped with seatbelts and age-appropriate restraints.
  6. Two-Adult Rule (SG-POL-002) confirmed for every vehicle. Two approved adults per vehicle is the standard.
Exceptions
One-Adult Transport Exceptions

One-adult transport is permitted only when:

  • The driver is transporting their own child or household family member.
  • The ministry leader gives explicit same-day approval AND the parent/guardian has provided written permission (SG-FRM-009) for that specific trip.
Any other single-adult transport of unrelated minors requires pre-approved emergency documentation, logged in SG-OPS-001 (Exception Log) and reviewed by the Safeguard Coordinator.
On the Road
During the Trip
  • Obey all traffic laws at all times.
  • All passengers wear seatbelts. Age-appropriate child restraints required per Alberta law.
  • No handheld mobile device use while driving.
  • No private 1:1 texting or messaging with minors about transportation. All communication must include a parent or second leader.
  • Supervision ratios (SG-POL-002) apply during the activity, not just in the vehicle.
  • Trip coordinator must have medical/emergency information (from SG-FRM-005, Child Registration) for every participant.
Post-Trip
After the Trip
  1. Confirm all participants accounted for upon return.
  2. Complete the return section of SG-FRM-011 (Transportation Log).
  3. Report any incidents or variances using SG-FRM-006 (Incident Report) or SG-OPS-001 (Exception Log).
  4. Submit completed trip log to the ministry leader or Safeguard Coordinator.
Overnight
Off-Site & Overnight Events
  • Same-gender adult supervision required for overnight accommodations and any situation involving changing, showers, or sleeping arrangements.
  • Adults must never share sleeping quarters with unrelated minors.
  • All overnight events require signed parent permission (SG-FRM-009) and a completed trip plan (SG-FRM-011).

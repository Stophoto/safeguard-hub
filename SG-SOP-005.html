SG-SOP-005  Standard Operating Procedure

Receiving & Releasing Children

Authorized adults only. Accurate records. Every time.
Document Code: SG-SOP-005 Version: 2.0 Issued: March 2026
Children are received and released only to authorized adults, with accurate records every time. No exceptions.
Check-In
Receiving (Check-In)
  1. Greet child and parent/guardian.
  2. Confirm child’s name and class placement.
  3. Parent/guardian completes check-in for the child.
  4. Note any allergies, medical needs, or special instructions.
  5. Confirm ratios are not exceeded before admitting child.
Ongoing
During Program
  1. Keep sign-in sheet accessible to leaders.
  2. Maintain custody awareness — children do not leave the room without leader approval.
  3. Record any incidents or exceptions as they occur.
Check-Out
Releasing (Check-Out)
  1. Confirm the adult is on the authorized pickup list (from SG-FRM-005, Child Registration).
  2. Ask for ID if the adult is unfamiliar to the leader.
  3. Parent/guardian completes check-out for the child.
  4. Note any late pickups or unusual circumstances.
  5. Leader initials release for accountability.
Rules
Quick Rules
  • Never release a child to someone not listed by the parent/guardian.
  • If there is any uncertainty, keep the child with you and contact the ministry leader immediately.
  • All custody exceptions must be recorded in SG-OPS-001 (Admin Guide, Exception Log).

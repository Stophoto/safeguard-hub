SG-FRM-003 · Police Check

Your Police Information Check.

A Police Information Check (PIC) is required before you serve with children, youth, or vulnerable adults. Valid for 3 years. Bethany Chapel covers the cost.
—
—

How the Police Check works

  1. Pick up the consent form (SG-FRM-003) from the Safeguard Coordinator.
  2. Take it to a local RCMP / police detachment along with two pieces of ID.
  3. Submit the check — Bethany Chapel covers the fee; bring the church letter included with the form.
  4. Results are sent directly to the Coordinator, who reviews them and updates your record.
  5. Record your submission below so your dashboard reflects that you're waiting on results.
Already submitted elsewhere? If you have a recent Police Check (within the last 3 years) on file with another organization, the Coordinator may be able to accept it. Talk to them directly.

Record your submission

Once you've handed in the consent form to the police, enter the date you submitted it below. The Coordinator will mark it cleared when the results come back.
